Welcome to the Time Capsule Network

The Time Capsule Network is a decentralized platform designed to preserve your memories, ideas, and digital assets securely on the blockchain. Our platform allows you to create digital time capsules that can be locked away and opened on a future date, providing a unique way to pass messages, artifacts, and tokens through time.

Features

  • Governance: Participate in the governance of the Time Capsule Network and help shape its future.
  • Tokens: Utilize our native tokens to create and interact with capsules, enabling a tokenomics system that rewards participation and engagement.
  • Capsules: Create your own time capsules with customizable unlock dates, content, and recipients.
  • Capsule Management: Manage your capsules easily with our Capsule Management System. Find, create, transfer, and follow capsules with just a few clicks.

Getting Started

To begin creating and interacting with time capsules, simply click on the "Login" button to authenticate with your Internet Identity. Once logged in, you can start exploring the Time Capsule Network, create your first capsule, or participate in governance and community activities.

Join us in preserving the past and embracing the future, one capsule at a time.
